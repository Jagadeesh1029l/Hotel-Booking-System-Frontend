About Aruna Hotel

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Aruna Hotel has established itself as a premier destination for travelers seeking comfort, luxury, and exceptional service. What began as a modest establishment has grown into a renowned hotel chain with properties across major cities.

Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has earned us numerous accolades in the hospitality industry, and we continue to innovate and improve our services to exceed guest expectations.

Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy

Terms & Conditions

1. Reservations and Check-In

1.1. A valid identification card is required at check-in.

1.2. Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM.

1.3. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

1.4. Reservations are confirmed upon receipt of a deposit or credit card guarantee.

2. Cancellation Policy

2.1. Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled arrival date will receive a full refund.

2.2. Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the scheduled arrival date will be charged one night's stay.

2.3. No-shows will be charged for the full reservation amount.

3. Payment

3.1. All rates are quoted in local currency and are subject to applicable taxes.

3.2. Accepted payment methods include major credit cards and cash.

3.3. A credit card is required upon check-in for incidental charges.

4. Hotel Rules

4.1. No smoking is allowed in the rooms or indoor public areas.

4.2. Pets are not allowed unless explicitly stated as a pet-friendly room.

4.3. The hotel is not responsible for loss of valuables. Use the in-room safe for storing valuables.

4.4. Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal information such as name, address, email, phone number, and payment information.

1.2. Information about your stay including preferences, special requests, and feedback.

1.3. Information collected through cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1. To process your reservation and provide the services you requested.

2.2. To communicate with you about your stay or future promotions.

2.3. To improve our services and customer experience.

2.4. For legal and security purposes.

3. Information Sharing

3.1. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3.2. We may share your information with service providers who help us deliver our services.

3.3. We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

4.1. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

4.2. We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

5. Your Rights

5.1. You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information.

5.2. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

5.3. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@arunahotel.com.
